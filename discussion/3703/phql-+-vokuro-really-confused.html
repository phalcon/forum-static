This is called a 'Finder':

$confirmation = EmailConfirmations::findFirstByCode($code);

It's the same as:

$confirmation = EmailConfirmations::findFirst(array("code = ?0", 'bind' => array($code)));

Model EmailConfirmations has a field called 'code'
