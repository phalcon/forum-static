Don't have your exact table structure to test at the moment. But query should look like this:

SELECT
    N.id, N.created_at
FROM news_categories_relation AS R
LEFT JOIN news AS N ON N.id = R.news_id
WHERE R.news_category_id <> 3
GROUP BY N.id
ORDER BY N.id DESC
