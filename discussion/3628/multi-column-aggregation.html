What do you mean by model? PHQL is also part of Model/ORM.

You can use the query builder if you don't want to use PHQL.

$robots = $this->modelsManager->createBuilder()
    ->columns('name', 'count(id)', 'count(distinct person)')
    ->from('MyModel')
    ->where('grade>=3') 
    ->getQuery()
    ->execute();
