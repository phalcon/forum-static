We're putting the final touches on the second major new release of Phalcon. Each new release brings with itself a new look highlighting the most important features of the framework and its present. This time we want to give the opportunity to community members to help us design the new look to be used in the Phalcon 2 lifecycle.

We're looking for an enthusiast graphic designer/agency/company who want to contribute a new look for Phalcon when version 2 is released.

If you want to participate, please submit your proposal here allowing the community to provide feedback.

Consider the following before submit the design:

  • A cool design that permeates the love we feel for this framework <3
  • Show an "Everything you need" section showing the components provided by the framework in the main page
  • Show an "Our amazing contributors" section showing who is contributing to the project
  • Highlight Zephir as base language

Phalcon's official website has over 14,000 monthly visits and according to Alexa it's one of the 25.000 most visited websites in the world. By contributing with this design you can add a "Designed by" badge to the website referring customers and people interested in your work.

Please submit a screenshoot of your proposal in this thread before October 1st, 2014.

UPDATE: Time is over, vote your favorite design here

Thank you!

Thanks CichyEll! Time for a small case study...

Current Phalcon logo has certain weak points which i tried to eliminate while doing my logo version. In order of importance (the upper are the most important):

  1. A healthy and happy bird always has feathers which do not stick out too much. If they do so, the bird has definitively got some trouble. The bird who looses feathers is definitively in a BIG trouble.

  2. A good logo is never too complex (not the case of current Phalcon logo).

  3. The current phalcon bird is looking and moving in forward-down direction which means progress but negative menthal state (which logically combines with phalcon's disheveled look, oh poor bird!)

  4. A trendy logo will be threated as eye candy only until it comes out of trend. The current logo uses a very trendy "flat triangle" approach which is now very popular (just look at default background for many Samsung phones and tabs).

What could be done to keep the current logo's trendy-sharp-triangled look while eliminating the most critical troubles?

  1. Less complexity, more simplicity. And no stick out. Something like an origami bird could be a good idea (although very-very trendy again).

  2. The bird could be re-oriented. A good idea is a phalcon who flies to the top-right direction (which means progress and positive menthal state).

Why doesn't my logo totally follow these suggestions? Because i'm not sure that Phalcon deserves a trendy (which authomatically means a short-lifetime) logo. In a couple of years the "flat triangle" look will for sure become outdated, and i want to believe that it will not be the case for the Phalcon project.

The beauty of statistics is that you can prove and disprove almost anything by biasing your sample. In this case if we take the number of people/developers based on a population basis of each country:

USA has the most people (around 310m) so it is at the top. Russia has 143m so it should be second etc. Dominican republic has 10m people and it is second.

This is what is called an oddity in statistics.

We had a similar situation when we tried to introduce Phalcon to cPanel. (see this post: https://www.niden.net/2013/06/voting-for-phalcon-as-cpanel-feature.html) Initially we were number 2 in the feature request list. But cPanel in all its wisdom decided to remove all the votes because they all came from the same source, which was our blog post. Now after all this time cPanel did release the module and it's great that they did but the point is that one single source of traffic does skew results.

I really don't want to take anything from Max's design, it is gorgeous and so are the other submitted designs. I really like the fact that Max and his company took the liberty of promoting Phalcon to the user base in DR. Kudos Sir!

I guess we need to talk about this and make a decision soon.
