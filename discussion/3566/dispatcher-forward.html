It's not a good practice use exceptions to control the flow of the application. Exceptions must be used for exceptional cases. You can use a dispatcher forward to execute another action or call the appropiate handler in that case: http://docs.phalcon.io/en/latest/reference/dispatching.html#forwarding-to-other-actions
