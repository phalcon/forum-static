try this

{% raw %}{{{% endraw %} [1, 2, 3]|join(' | ') {% raw %}}}{% endraw %}

Output

1 | 2 | 3

Also try this

{% raw %}{{{% endraw %} 1 " join with " 2 " join with " 3{% raw %}}}{% endraw %}

Output

1 join with 2 join with 3

Explanation

Join with " [anything you want to join with] "
