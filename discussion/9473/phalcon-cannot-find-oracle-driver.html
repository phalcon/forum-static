Finally found an answer on stack: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/21936091/how-to-install-oracle-instantclient-and-pdo-oci-on-ubuntu-machine Scroll down to the response that deals with pulling down the source of your version of PHP. I followed those steps, with 2 exceptions:

  1. 12.1 is a listed version in my PHP, so no need to add it to the config.m3
  2. I had to add --with-pdo-oci=instantclient,/path/to/instantclient_12_1,12.1 to the ./configure command other wise, worked great and i'm up and running!
