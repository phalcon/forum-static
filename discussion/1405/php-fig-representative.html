Phalcon Framework is now part of PHP-FIG with Phalcon Team member Andres Gutierrez as representative. https://www.php-fig.org/.

Our participation will allow Phalcon to be more compatible with other frameworks and vice versa.

Feel free to open discussions in this forum regarding standards discussed there and also how our community could contribute more to the whole PHP community!
