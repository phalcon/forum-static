Hello.

It depends on the session adapter you use (see: http://phalcon-php-framework-documentation.readthedocs.org/en/latest/api/Phalcon_Session_Adapter.html) You can implement your own session adapter and control the life cycle inside it.

Note, session.gc-maxlifetime won't work! (see: http://php.net/manual/en/session.configuration.php#ini.session.gc-maxlifetime) There is a good explanation here http://stackoverflow.com/questions/520237/how-do-i-expire-a-php-session-after-30-minutes

Please, describe here which session lifecycle you need and I will try to help
