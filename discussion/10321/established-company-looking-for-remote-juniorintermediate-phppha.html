Company Information

Max Value Software, LLC. is the world largest provider of third-party online poker tools built specifically for online poker players Our leading software and entities are Hold'em Manager, PokerTracker, CardRunners, TableNinja and more. Max Value Software (MVS) was formed in 2014 through the merger of PokerTracker Software, LLC and CardRunners Gaming, Inc. PokerTracker came to market in 2001 and Hold'em Manager in 2006. We are a Limited Liability Company registered in Delaware, United States; however, our staffing is worldwide from Japan to Ireland to Canada and to our home the eastern United States.

Opportunity Available

Our three main web properties were built independetly from one another and are becoming slightly outdated. We have begun consolidating various portions of our properties into cohesive units/projects leveraging the Phalcon framework. The developer hired will need to help maintain our old environments until they are eventually transitioned to Phalcon as well as have the opportunity to help build out these new environments leveraging the Phalcon framework.

100% of this work will be completed remotely; however, we are only looking for developers in the GMT-1 to GMT +8 timezones in order to be on the same general schedule as the rest of our development team. Actual hours of work are flexible but we are looking for at least 30 hours per week and the developer should be availabe for a few hours daily Monday-Friday in order to maintain efficient communication.

Requirements

  • 2+ Years of Experience with PHP, MySQL, HTML5 & JS
  • Understands and codes in OOP
  • Worked on at least one project leveraging the Phalcon framework
  • Good communication in English
  • Lives in a timezone between GMT -1 through GMT +8 and can be available Monday-Friday for a few hours for communication.
  • Available for at least 30 hours per week.
  • Knowledge of poker is NOT required

Technologies Used

The following technologies will be used. Some experience with the following are beneficial but not required.

  • PhpStorm (IDE of choice)
  • Perforce (Source Code Management)
  • Vagrant (For local VM development)
  • Jira (For project & task management)
  • Slack (How we communicate)

How to Apply

Please send all inquiries and applications to devjobs (at) maxvaluesoftware.com

When applying, please provide us with your resume, a URL to a project that you were involved with that is built on the Phalcon framework and a short paragraph outlining your responsibilities for the project.

We are hoping to have this position filled within the next few weeks. Once the position has been filled, we will update this thread indicating as such therefore keep your applications coming!
