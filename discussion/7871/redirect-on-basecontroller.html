There are two approaches for this.

  • Forwarding - https://docs.phalcon.io/en/latest/reference/dispatching.html#forwarding-to-other-actions
  • Redirection - https://docs.phalcon.io/en/latest/reference/response.html#making-redirections

This is an explanation between differences https://stackoverflow.com/questions/21301624/phalcon-redirection-and-forwarding

Also forward is void method, so you do not have use return.
