Hi Dan!

You can add any attributes, just like that

{% raw %}{{{% endraw %} form('session/start', "method": "post", "data-abide": ""{% raw %}}}{% endraw %}

In HTML it will be format like normal form with

<form action="/session/start" method="post" data-abide="">
