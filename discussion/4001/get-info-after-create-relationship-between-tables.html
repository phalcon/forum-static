You can use a many-to-many relation here.

$this->hasManyToMany(
            "teamID",
            "TeamsLeagues",
            "teamID", "leagueID",
            "Leagues",
            "leagueID",
            array(
                "alias" => "teamsLeagues"
            )
        );

I think this should work and you can access your property as shown in your example.
