Phalcon can work as full-stack framework, thus you are not limited to the official approach but go with the solution that works best for your project.

See PhalconEye CMS - it's using HMVC approach (in some sort) and might give you and idea. https://github.com/lantian/PhalconEye/tree/master/app/modules
