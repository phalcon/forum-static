In your index.volt (directly in your view dir) or in your layout-view, insert {% raw %}{{{% endraw %}flashSession.output(){% raw %}}}{% endraw %}

Then both of them will get show when you output {% raw %}{{{% endraw %}content(){% raw %}}}{% endraw %} in a view.
