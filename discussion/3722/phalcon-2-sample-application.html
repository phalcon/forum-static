Phalcon 2 will work as Phalcon 1.x, current tutorials/samples will work for you in one version or another:

  • INVO (Acl, Pagination, CRUD): https://github.com/phalcon/invo
  • Vokuro (User and Admin modules, Security, Forms, Validation): https://github.com/phalcon/vokuro
  • Phosphorum (Cache, Pagination, ORM, Relationships, Ajax, Integration with Third-Party services): https://github.com/phalcon/forum

More samples: https://docs.phalcon.io/en/2.0.0/reference/samples.html
