You could use Phalcons custom routes - https://docs.phalcon.io/en/latest/reference/routing.html#usage-examples

https://example.com/api/Something to https://example.com/api/Something.json

$router->add("/api/:controller\.json", array(
    'module'      => 'api',
    'controller'  => 1,
    'file'        => 2
));
