hi @grizour are you minify html? maybe this regexp helps you


 preg_replace(['#(?ix)(?>[^\S ]\s*|\s{2,})(?=(?:(?:[^<]++|<(?!/?(?:textarea|pre|script)\b))*+)(?:<(?>textarea|pre|script)\b|\z))#',
                '#>\s+<#'],
                [' ', '><'],
                $content);

This prevent minification in textarea, pre and script tags Good luck
