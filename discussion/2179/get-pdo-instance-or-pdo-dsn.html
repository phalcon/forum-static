From inside a controller:


//resolve a singleton service named 'db' from our dependency injector
$db_service = $this->getShared('db');
//resolve the handler of the DB service (PDO in this case)
$pdo = $db->getInternalHandler();

$sql = $pdo->prepare('select ......');
//so on with regular pdo syntax
....

Any reason you need to resolve PDO in particular? Phalcon provides a pretty good abstraction on top of PDO which is a lot less verbose than regular PDO. Refer to its syntax here http://docs.phalcon.io/en/latest/api/Phalcon_Db_Adapter_Pdo.html
